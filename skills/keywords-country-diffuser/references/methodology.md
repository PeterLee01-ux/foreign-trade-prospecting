# Prospecting Methodology

## Search construction

Use this structure and translate the quoted terms into the target country's primary language:

```text
("scent machine" OR "scent diffuser" OR "aroma diffuser machine" OR
 "fragrance diffuser" OR "cold air scent diffuser" OR
 "waterless scent diffuser" OR "nebulizing scent diffuser" OR
 "HVAC scent diffuser" OR "commercial scenting system" OR LOCAL_PRODUCT_TERMS)
AND
(distributor OR importer OR wholesaler OR dealer OR retailer OR supplier OR
 "scent marketing company" OR "home fragrance company" OR "hotel supplier" OR
 LOCAL_BUYER_TERMS)
AND "COUNTRY"
```

Run narrower variants for professional scenting, HVAC scenting, home fragrance, hotel supply, beauty/wellness distribution, and local B2B directories.

## Match scoring

- 90–100: sells, rents, installs, distributes, or services professional cold-air/nebulizing/HVAC scent systems.
- 80–89: strong commercial scenting, scent-marketing, room-fragrance, or specialized distributor fit.
- 70–79: relevant fragrance, wellness, hotel, beauty, gift, or private-label channel.
- 50–69: adjacent HVAC, odor-control, fragrance manufacturing, or broad wholesale company requiring verification.

Raise scores for named B2B services, professional diffuser products, HVAC integration, distributor language, multiple business sectors, and named contacts. Lower scores for consumer-only retail, passive reed diffusers only, ambiguous `diffuser` terminology, inactive sites, or uncertain local entities.

## Contact treatment

Keep all publicly listed contact data. Separate named/direct contacts from public/company contacts when possible.

Highlight red:

- generic mailbox local parts including `info`, `sales`, `sale`, `office`, `contact`, `hello`, `bonjour`, `commercial`, `service`, `support`, and local-language equivalents;
- country-specific toll-free, public-service, shared switchboard, or hotline patterns.

Do not label contacts as verified unless confirmed on the cited official source. Public appearance and syntax checks do not prove deliverability.

## Office districts

Include nationally important cities plus representative regional and smaller commercial centers. Record recognized CBDs, business parks, office corridors, luxury retail/hotel streets, convention areas, airports, and fragrance/beauty clusters. Treat these streets as search aids, not proof that a prospect is located there.

## Quality checks

- No duplicate company/domain rows unless a distinct regional or named contact justifies them.
- No guessed email, phone, person, address, company size, or revenue.
- Every company row has a source URL.
- Generic contacts and hotlines remain present and red-marked.
- Match-tier and score agree with the evidence.
- All sheets are filterable, readable, frozen at the header, and visually inspected.
